Sassy Cupcakes Gourmet Bakery

For the past three years, our bakery has grown from a custom catering operation in the owner's kitchen to an award-winning commercial bakery with more than 20 employees. We recently opened a cupcake shop to help satisfy drop-in cravings, and we didn't just settle for cupcakes! Enjoy a fresh-mad brownie of the day, a fresh-fruit smoothie, hot or iced coffee, cake pops, and other delights that we whip up in our test kitchens.
