The Duet/Trio Competition will be held Friday, April 11, at approx. 5:00 p.m. The entry fee for Duet’s is $40.00 per girl ($80.00); and the entree fee for Trio’s is $40.00 per girl ($120.00). These fees are non-refundable. If you are participating in the Solo Competition as well, we will arrange performance times so that you are able to compete in both categories. The routine may consist of any style of dance. Emphasis is on flexibility, showmanship, technique, entertainment and overall effect. The time limit for each routine will be 1-3 minutes. You will only have one chance to perform after your music has started; you cannot start your music over. Each contestant will receive a trophy.
