Everyday Favorites

These delicious started out as new weekly experiments, but quickly became everyday fan favorites! They are available in our shop every day, from 11 a.m. to whenever they run out. Small cupcakes are $2 each and large are $3 each.

Black-and-white Bombshell

Oreo cookies in the cake and in the frosting, filled with swiss buttercream & topped with chocolate drizzle and cookie pieces

Confetti

Rainbow sprinkles melted in vanilla cake, topped with vanilla buttercream and more rainbow sprinkles

Cookie Dough Delight

Chocolate cake with cookie dough baked inside, topped with vanilla buttercream and a homemade chocolate chip cookie.

Elvis Lives!

Banana cake and peanut butter-cream cheese frosting, dusted with crushed pistachios.

Goddess Nutella

Chocolate cake filled with Nutella, topped with Vanilla-Nutella frosting, and crowned with Ferraro-Rocher candy.

Peanut Butter Cup

Rich chocolate cake, peanut butter frosting (not too thick ~ not too creamy!), hand-dipped in chocolate ganache, topped with a chocolate ganache flowers.

Red Dahlia

Our version of traditional Red Velvet cake, topped with cream cheese frosting and red glitter.

Salted Caramel on Chocolate

Chocolate cake, topped with buttercream made from house-made caramel and a tiny bit of added sea salt. Topped with chocolate and toffee bits.

The Retro

Chocolate cake, filled and topped with swiss buttercream, then dipped in chocolate ganache and augmented with a little squiggle
