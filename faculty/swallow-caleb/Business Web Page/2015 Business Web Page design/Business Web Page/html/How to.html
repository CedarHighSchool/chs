How to Load a Pez Dispenser

  1. Gather the necessary items
    • Pez dispenser
    • Package of Pez candy
  2. Unwrap Pez Candy
  3. Grasp stack of candy between thumb and forefinger
  4. Fully extend dispenser
  5. Load candy into dispenser
  6. Enjoy
Pez Dispenser:
a plastic candy dispenser that holds a verticle stack of bite sized delicious candy. Candy is dispensed one at a time by lifting the head of the character on top.
