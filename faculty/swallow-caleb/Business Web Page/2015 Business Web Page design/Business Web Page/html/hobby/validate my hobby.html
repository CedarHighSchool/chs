I like to can food at home

People who like my hobby are

  • mostly women
  • people who like to know where their food comes from
  • people who work really hard
  • People who like to modify their own food without the store preservatives or allergens

I like to can because I enjoy using fresh vegetables. I think fresh food tastes much better than what is in a can. I used to think that canning saved money, though I'm not sure that is really true anymore. I do buy some things at case lot sales, but I have certain recipes that I think taste better homemade. I can mostly green beans and tomatoes (salsa, diced tomatoes, and sauced.) I have also canned pickles, peaches, pears, applesauce and apple pie filling.
