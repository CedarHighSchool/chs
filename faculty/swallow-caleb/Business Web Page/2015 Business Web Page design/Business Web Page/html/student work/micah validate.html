Sabre

People who like the Sabre
  • Civil war enthusiasts
  • Weapon collectors
The 1860 sabre is a weapon known as old wrist breaker due to the weight and lack of balance. The proper sharpening technique is to only sharpen the upper portion of the blade. The best way to keep the blade from becoming damaged is to block with the lower unsharpened edge and keep a light coat of oil on it.
